A LITTLE ABOUT ME



I'm Ezra. I'm 28 years old, I ride a bike, and I'm a Dominica-Jew. I specialize in leading teams to accomplish great things, while having fun together. I'm well known for my clever use of gifs in emails. When I discovered Product Management, I knew it was my calling. By combining deep business problem solving, creativity, technology, and teamwork, as Product Managers we just get to do cool stuff with our teams.
